Lasagna

Home

Description

Spoon meat sauce on the bottom of a lightly greased casserole dish. Add 4 boiled lasagna noodles (or use no-boil or fresh noodles). Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture on top. Add 1.5 cups of meat sauce. Repeat the same layers two more times. Top with mozzarella cheese. Cover and bake at 375Â° for 30 minutes. Remove cover and bake for 15 more minutes. Broil at the end if desired. Let it rest for 15 minutes prior to serving with Garlic Bread With Cheese.

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef: 1 pound (454 g)
  • Hot Breakfast Sausage: 1 pound (454 g)
  • Cottage Cheese: 3 cups (710 g)
  • Mozzarella Cheese: 1 pound (454 g)
  • Parmesan Cheese: 1 cup (113 g)
  • Lasagna Noodles: 1 (10-oz) package (283 g)
  • Whole Tomatoes: 2 (14.5-oz) cans (794 g)
  • Tomato Paste: 2 (6-oz) cans (340 g)
  • Dried Parsley: 4 tablespoons (14 g)
  • Dried Basil: 2 tablespoons (6 g)
  • Salt: 2 1/2 teaspoons (15 g)
  • Olive Oil: 1 tablespoon (15 ml)
  • Garlic: 2 cloves (6 g)
  • Eggs: 2 (113 g)

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
